Our Mission Through Partnership

Film From Zero thrives through the generous support of industry leaders, educational institutions, technology companies, and cultural organizations. Our Partnership Board represents a diverse coalition of allies committed to democratizing filmmaking education and creating opportunities for emerging creators from all backgrounds.

Partnership Impact

Our equipment partnership program has provided over $500,000 worth of professional gear to emerging filmmakers, removing financial barriers to creation.

Industry Connections

Partner mentorship programs have resulted in 200+ internships, 50+ job placements, and countless professional networking opportunities.
