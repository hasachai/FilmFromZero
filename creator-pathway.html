Stage 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4)

What You'll Learn

  • Fundamentals of visual storytelling
  • Basic film terminology and concepts
  • Introduction to different film genres
  • Film analysis and critical viewing
  • Industry overview and career paths

Activities & Assignments

  • Watch and analyze classic short films
  • Complete "Intro to Filmmaking" workshop
  • Write your first one-page film treatment
  • Join community discussion groups

Stage 2: Skills Development (Weeks 5-12)

What You'll Learn

  • Screenwriting for short films
  • Basic cinematography and camera work
  • Lighting fundamentals
  • Sound recording and design basics
  • Introduction to editing software

Activities & Assignments

  • Complete 3 core workshops of your choice
  • Create short practice videos (30-60 seconds)
  • Collaborate on group projects
  • Receive feedback from mentors

Stage 3: Pre-Production (Weeks 13-16)

What You'll Learn

  • Script development and refinement
  • Storyboarding and shot planning
  • Casting and working with actors
  • Location scouting and permissions
  • Budget planning and resource management

Activities & Assignments

  • Write your final short film script (5-10 pages)
  • Create detailed storyboards
  • Develop production timeline
  • Form your production team

Stage 4: Production (Weeks 17-20)

What You'll Learn

  • On-set leadership and direction
  • Working with cast and crew
  • Problem-solving under pressure
  • Quality control and continuity
  • Safety protocols and best practices

Activities & Assignments

  • Shoot your 5-10 minute short film
  • Daily production reports and reflection
  • Mentor check-ins and support
  • Document behind-the-scenes content

Stage 5: Post-Production (Weeks 21-24)

What You'll Learn

  • Advanced editing techniques
  • Color correction and grading
  • Sound mixing and audio post
  • Title design and credits
  • Export settings and distribution prep

Activities & Assignments

  • Complete your short film edit
  • Receive feedback and make revisions
  • Create promotional materials
  • Prepare for final showcase
