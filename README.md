# Film From Zero

A comprehensive platform for aspiring filmmakers to start their journey from zero to creating professional short films. Film From Zero provides free workshops, guided creator pathways, mentorship opportunities, and a supportive community network.

## About Film From Zero

Film From Zero democratizes filmmaking education by providing completely free access to professional-level training, equipment, and mentorship. Our mission is to remove barriers to filmmaking education and create opportunities for creators from all backgrounds.

## Features

### 🎓 Free Workshops
- Comprehensive workshop series covering all aspects of filmmaking
- Beginner to advanced levels
- Online, in-person, and hybrid formats
- Expert instructors from the film industry

### 🎬 Creator Pathway
- Structured 24-week program guiding learners from beginner to short film creation
- Progressive skill building across 5 stages
- Dedicated mentorship throughout the journey
- Equipment access and professional showcase opportunities

### 👥 Mentor & Alumni Network
- One-on-one mentorship with industry professionals
- Success stories and inspiration from program graduates
- Ongoing support and professional networking
- Opportunities for alumni to become mentors

### 🤝 Partnership Board
- Network of industry allies and supporters
- Equipment access through partner organizations
- Distribution and exhibition opportunities
- Educational institution partnerships

## Call to Action

Every page ends with three key actions:
- **Join Film From Zero** - Become part of our community
- **Submit Your Idea** - Share your creative vision
- **Start From Nothing** - Begin your filmmaking journey

## Website Structure

- `index.html` - Homepage with overview and features
- `workshops.html` - Free workshop catalog and information
- `creator-pathway.html` - Detailed 24-week pathway program
- `mentor-network.html` - Mentorship program and success stories
- `partnerships.html` - Partner network and collaboration opportunities
- `styles.css` - Responsive CSS styling

## Technologies Used

- HTML5 for semantic structure
- CSS3 with responsive design
- Mobile-first approach
- Accessible design principles
