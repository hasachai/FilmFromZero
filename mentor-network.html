Mentor Requirements

  • Minimum 5 years professional film industry experience
  • Demonstrated success in your field
  • Passion for teaching and mentoring
  • Commitment to regular mentee interactions
  • Alignment with Film From Zero values

Time Commitment

  • 1-2 hours per month per mentee
  • Monthly video call sessions
  • Email/message support as needed
  • Optional: Participate in workshops or events
  • Minimum 6-month commitment
